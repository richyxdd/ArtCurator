About ArtCurator

Discover. Curate. Experience.

ArtCurator is your personal space to explore, save, and curate beautiful works of art. Our mission is to make art more accessible by letting you create your own galleries, view them anytime, and deepen your appreciation for creativity from around the world.

As a special bonus, active gallery creators get exclusive discounts on tickets to top art museums in major cities like New York, Boston, and Chicago.
